All About Me

By Connie Huang

Some of my favorite things to do include hiking, watching TV, and working out. After a long day of school, however, I’ll usually cool down and take a nap. I love playing flute in the wind ensemble at Oak Ridge and going to marching practice every week, but the best part of band are the football games because there is so much hype and we can really get into the school spirit.

My favorite places to eat or hang out with friends are In n Out, Juice it Up and the Nugget.
